Mustang Sally is a 2006 horror film starring Elizabeth Daily, Mark Anthony Parrish, Lindsey Labrum, Don Wallace, Erik Fellows and Al Santos. Six friends think that they are going to a house of prostitution, but encounter danger instead. The title is a reference to the rhythm and blues tune "Mustang Sally", popularized by Wilson Pickett and later featured in several movies. It also has jazz score by trumpeter Terence Blanchard.
